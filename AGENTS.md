# Project Conventions

Use convention indexes as the entry points for project conventions.
When paired English and Korean documents conflict, choose the intended policy and synchronize both documents.

- [Documentation Convention Index](docs/en/index.md)

## Planning Documents

When changing feature requirements, product behavior, or long-lived product policies, check the relevant planning documents under `plans/`.
Keep planning documents synchronized with core product behavior whether the change starts from planning, implementation, or review.
Planning documents capture what and why; code captures how; static analysis, tests, type checks, generators, and CI verify concrete behavior.
